INTRODUCTION
College is not only a place for career enlightenment but a place to create unforgettable memories and friends. It's a phase in our lives that teaches the importance of living your life rather than just existing in it. Events add the climax to our college time.
STORY
But not everything happens as expected. Just like in every movie, this story also has some drama. The events from which students had high expectations started to collapse like a sandcastle. The events which were supposed to be a memory for a lifetime began to fade. Poor management and overshadowing of the talent caused further agitation among the students. At that point, few students with a zeal to rise above the level of the ordinary decided to get what's due to them and lead to the formation of an intercollegiate event organizing team, team Audspire. 
OBJECTIVE
With the vision of organizing meaningful and alluring college programs, team Audspire is here to equip students with the biggest platform of events. We aspire to create such a mesh of networks that's never been established before. Our reign of control won't be limited to a single college nor a zone, we wish to spread our support in the entire nation with our technical excellence in the fields of attracting sponsors, summoning influencers, and organizing events of all sorts. With firm determination and eyes set on the goal, we are just a few laps away from setting a milestone of being the most pronounced event organizing team.
